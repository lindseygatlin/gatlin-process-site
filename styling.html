CTA: Call To Action

For my site I have chosen to have three primary CTAs: Sign In, Get Involved, and Request a Meal. The most important of these is Request a Meal. This is the action that will direct those in need towards a form that will allow them to get connected with the resources needed for their situation. This CTA will also be a bolder color to create more emphasis. The secondary CTA is Get Involved which will allow users who would like to give of their time or money to learn more about the organization and proceed to the desired route. This CTA will be beside the Request a Meal but without color to make it less prominent. The final CTA is the Sign In button beside the hamburger menu for returning users. This one will use color to be easily visible even at a smaller scale.
